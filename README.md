A simple web application for managing wedding dance lessons, focused on scheduling, client management, and automated reminders.

---

## MVP Scope

### Calendar and Appointments (Core Feature)
- Separate calendar for each instructor  
- Lesson bookings with time slots  
- Prevention of overlapping appointments  
- Basic Google Calendar synchronization to avoid double booking  

This is the core functionality of the application.

---

### Client Profile (Couple)
Each couple has a dedicated profile containing:
- Contact details  
- Wedding date  
- Chosen song  
- Dance style  
- Internal notes (progress, preferences, observations)

Acts as a lightweight CRM for the dance school.

---

### SMS Notifications
Automated reminders sent to clients:
- 24 hours before the lesson  
- 2 hours before the lesson  

Helps reduce missed appointments.

---

## Phase 2 / Extras

### Reports
- Simple overview of scheduled lessons  
- Basic statistics per school and per instructor  

---

### Website to App Synchronization
- Bookings made on the website appear automatically in the application calendar  
- Eliminates manual data entry  

---

### Scheduled Messages
Additional automated SMS messages:
- Encouragement messages  
- Review requests  
- Post-wedding congratulations  

---

## Notes
- The MVP is intentionally small and fast to build  
- Designed to solve real scheduling and communication problems  
- Easily extensible into CRM, marketing, and analytics features  
- Suitable for single schools, with potential for scaling or white-label use
